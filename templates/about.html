About PractiCheck

Empowering students, lecturers, and supervisors with smart practicum management solutions.

Our Mission

PractiCheck aims to simplify practicum management in universities by providing a seamless platform for students, supervisors, and lecturers. Our goal is to track attendance, submissions, and progress efficiently while keeping everyone connected.

Our Technology

Built with Django, Bootstrap, and modern web technologies, PractiCheck leverages real-time updates, secure user management, and intuitive dashboards to provide the best experience for our users. We strive for simplicity, reliability, and performance.

Our Values

Innovation

We embrace new ideas to improve practicum management.

Integrity

We maintain trust through transparency and reliability.

Collaboration

We work closely with students and educators to achieve results.
